CirculantGraph

  • CirculantGraph[n,j] gives the circulant graph with n vertices and jump j Cn(j).
  • CirculantGraph[n,{j1,j2, … }] gives the circulant graph with n vertices and jumps j1, j2, … Cn(j1,j2, … ).

Attributes: NHoldAll, Protected, ReadProtected
