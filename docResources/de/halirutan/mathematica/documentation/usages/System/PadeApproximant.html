PadeApproximant

  • PadeApproximant[expr,{x,x0,{m,n}}] gives the Pad é approximant to expr about the point x=x0, with numerator order m and denominator order n.
  • PadeApproximant[expr,{x,x0,n}] gives the diagonal Pad é approximant to expr about the point x=x0 of order n.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
