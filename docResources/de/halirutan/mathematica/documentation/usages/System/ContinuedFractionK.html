ContinuedFractionK

  • ContinuedFractionK[f,g,{i,imin,imax}] represents the continued fraction Κ i=imin imax f/g.
  • ContinuedFractionK[g,{i,imin,imax}] represents the continued fraction Κ i=imin imax 1/g.

Attributes: HoldAll, Protected, ReadProtected

Options: Assumptions, GenerateConditions, Method, VerifyConvergence
