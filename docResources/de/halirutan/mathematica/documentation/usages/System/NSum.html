NSum

  • NSum[f,{i,imin,imax}] gives a numerical approximation to the sum Underoverscript[ ∑ i=iminimax]f.
  • NSum[f,{i,imin,imax,di}] uses a step di in the sum.

Attributes: HoldAll, Protected

Options: AccuracyGoal, Compiled, EvaluationMonitor, Method, NSumTerms, PrecisionGoal, VerifyConvergence, WorkingPrecision
