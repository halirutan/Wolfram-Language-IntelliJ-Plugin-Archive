AudioBlockMap

  • AudioBlockMap[f,audio,dur] applies f to non-overlapping partitions of length t in audio.
  • AudioBlockMap[f,audio,{dur,offset}] applies f to partitions with offset d in audio.
  • AudioBlockMap[f,audio,{dur,offset,wfun}] applies f after applying wfun to partitions in audio.

Attributes:Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
