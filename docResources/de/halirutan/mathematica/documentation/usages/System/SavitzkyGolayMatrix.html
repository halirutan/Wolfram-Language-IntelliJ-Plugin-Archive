SavitzkyGolayMatrix

  • SavitzkyGolayMatrix[r,k] gives a matrix corresponding to a smoothing kernel of radius r for performing polynomial regression of degree k.
  • SavitzkyGolayMatrix[{r1,r2},{k1,k2}] gives a matrix for performing polynomial regression of degree k1 over a window of radius r1 along rows, and degree k2 over a window of radius r2 along columns.
  • SavitzkyGolayMatrix[r,k,n] gives a matrix for performing the nth derivative of a polynomial regression of degree k.
  • SavitzkyGolayMatrix[{r1,r2 ... },{k1,k2, ... }, ... ] gives an array using the specified parameters for each direction i.
