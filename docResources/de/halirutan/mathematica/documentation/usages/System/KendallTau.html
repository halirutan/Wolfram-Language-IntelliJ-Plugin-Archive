KendallTau

  • KendallTau[v1,v2] gives Kendall's rank correlation coefficient τ for the vectors v1 and v2.
  • KendallTau[m] gives Kendall's rank correlation coefficients τ for the matrix m.
  • KendallTau[m1,m2] gives Kendall's rank correlation coefficients τ for the matrices m1 and m2.
  • KendallTau[dist] gives Kendall's rank correlation matrix for the multivariate symbolic distribution dist.
  • KendallTau[dist,i,j] gives the (i,j)th Kendall rank correlation for the multivariate symbolic distribution dist.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
