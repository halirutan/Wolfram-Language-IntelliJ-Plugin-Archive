BeckmannDistribution

  • BeckmannDistribution[ μ 1, μ 2, σ 1, σ 2] represents the Beckmann distribution with means μ 1 and μ 2 and standard deviations σ 1 and σ 2.
  • BeckmannDistribution[ μ 1, μ 2, σ 1, σ 2, ρ ] represents the Beckmann distribution with means μ 1 and μ 2, standard deviations σ 1 and σ 2, and correlation ρ .

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
