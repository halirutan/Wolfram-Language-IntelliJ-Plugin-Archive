FindChannels

  • FindChannels[] gives a list of channels in your home area on the channel broker server.
  • FindChannels[None] gives a list of your unnamed channels.
  • FindChannels[All] gives a list of all channels owned by you.
  • FindChannels[form] gives a list of channels in your home area whose names match the string pattern form.
  • FindChannels["RowBox[{user, :, form}]"] gives a list of accessible channels in the home area of the specified user whose names match form.
  • FindChannels[/StyleBox[abspath, TI]] gives a list of channels whose names match the string pattern "/abspath".
  • FindChannels["https://..."] gives a list of channels whose names match the specified pattern representing a URI.
  • FindChannels[Anonymous] gives a list of unnamed channels that you can access.
  • FindChannels[Full] gives a list of all channels accessible to you.

Attributes:Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
