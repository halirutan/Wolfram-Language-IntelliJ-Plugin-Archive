TagBox

  • TagBox[boxes,tag] is a low-level box construct that displays as boxes but maintains tag to guide the interpretation of boxes on input.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: AutoDelete, BaseStyle, DefaultBaseStyle, DefaultTooltipStyle, DeleteWithContents, DeletionWarning, Editable, SelectWithContents, Selectable, StripWrapperBoxes, SyntaxForm, TagBoxNote, Tooltip, TooltipDelay, TooltipStyle
