InverseFourierSinTransform

  • InverseFourierSinTransform[expr, ω ,t] gives the symbolic inverse Fourier sine transform of expr.
  • InverseFourierSinTransform[expr,{ ω 1, ω 2, … },{t1,t2, … }] gives the multidimensional inverse Fourier sine transform of expr.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: Assumptions, GenerateConditions, FourierParameters
