SphericalPlot3D

  • SphericalPlot3D[r, θ , ϕ ] generates a 3D plot with a spherical radius r as a function of spherical coordinates θ and ϕ .
  • SphericalPlot3D[r,{ θ , θ min, θ max},{ ϕ , ϕ min, ϕ max}] generates a 3D spherical plot over the specified ranges of spherical coordinates.
  • SphericalPlot3D[{r1,r2, … },{ θ , θ min, θ max},{ ϕ , ϕ min, ϕ max}] generates a 3D spherical plot with multiple surfaces.
