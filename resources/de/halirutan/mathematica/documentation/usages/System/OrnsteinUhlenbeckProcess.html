OrnsteinUhlenbeckProcess

  • OrnsteinUhlenbeckProcess[ μ , σ , θ ] represents an Ornstein – Uhlenbeck process with long-term mean μ , volatility σ , and mean reversion speed θ .
  • OrnsteinUhlenbeckProcess[ μ , σ , θ ,x0] represents an Ornstein – Uhlenbeck process with initial condition x0.
