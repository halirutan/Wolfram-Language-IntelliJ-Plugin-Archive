FourierSinTransform

  • FourierSinTransform[expr,t, ω ] gives the symbolic Fourier sine transform of expr.
  • FourierSinTransform[expr,{t1,t2, … },{ ω 1, ω 2, … }] gives the multidimensional Fourier sine transform of expr.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Options: Assumptions, GenerateConditions, FourierParameters
