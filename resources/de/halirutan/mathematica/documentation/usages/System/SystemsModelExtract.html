SystemsModelExtract

  • SystemsModelExtract[sys,{in1, … }] extracts the subsystem of the systems model sys associated with inputs at position ini.
  • SystemsModelExtract[sys,{in1, … },{out1, … }] extracts the subsystem associated with inputs and outputs at positions ini and outi, respectively.
  • SystemsModelExtract[sys,{in1, … },{out1, … },{s1, … }] extracts the subsystem of the state-space model sys associated with inputs, outputs, and states at ini, outi, and si, respectively.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
