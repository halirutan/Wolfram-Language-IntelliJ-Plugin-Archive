HazardFunction

  • HazardFunction[dist,x] gives the hazard function for the symbolic distribution dist evaluated at x.
  • HazardFunction[dist,{x1,x2, … }] gives the multivariate hazard function for the symbolic distribution dist evaluated at {x1,x2, … }.
  • HazardFunction[dist] gives the hazard function as a pure function.

Attributes: Protected, ReadProtected

Symbol has no options.
